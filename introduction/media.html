movies

Everything Everywhere All At Once came out three months ago and I've watched it three-four times already. I do feel like this is the best movie made this decade if not ever... The story it told was extremely unique to Hollywood told in a way so fantastically. The multi-verse concept has been recently tried by everyone but the Daniels (directors Daniel Kwan & Daniel Scheinert) really swept all of their film-making contemporaries. Everyone involved in this movie was super talented please watch this movie you won't regret it.
